About

Ada is based in Los Angeles. One of her favorite things about living in a city like LA is being able to try various cuisines and unique dishes that she might otherwise never get the chance to see, smell, or taste.

Food has always been one of Ada's passions. What started off as snapping quick photos of food to post online has turned into a dedication to capture the layers of a burger, the drizzle of honey, and the vibrant colors of a dish.
